Chocolate Crepe Recipe

Indulge in the delightful world of chocolate crepes with this easy-to-follow recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Chocolate chips or chocolate sauce (optional, for filling)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add the eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Whisk until the batter is smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Pour a small amount of batter into the skillet and swirl it around to coat the bottom evenly.
  4. Cook for about 1-2 minutes until the crepe is set and the edges start to lift. Flip it over and cook for an additional 1 minute.
  5. Remove the crepe from the skillet, and if desired, add chocolate chips or chocolate sauce to the center.
  6. Roll up the crepe and serve warm. Repeat the process with the remaining batter.

Enjoy your homemade chocolate crepes!
